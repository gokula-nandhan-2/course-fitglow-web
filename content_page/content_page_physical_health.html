Ensure Your Body is Healthy and You are Physically Fit

Introduction

Physical health is a fundamental aspect of overall well-being. It encompasses the normal functioning of the body, how it grows, feels, and moves, and how you care for it through diet, exercise, and lifestyle choices. This page provides insights into what physical health is, its importance, and tips on maintaining it.

What is Physical Health? Physical health can be defined as the normal functioning of the body. Representing one dimension of total well-being, it's about how your body grows, feels and moves, how you care for it, and what you put into it.

Why is Physical Well-being Important?

  • Keeping healthy and active reduces all-risk mortality by 30%.
  • Eating well helps balance metabolism and improve digestion.
  • Improving hydration helps physical and mental health.
  • Sleeping better allows your body and mind to recharge.
  • Regular activity will improve flexibility, strengthen muscles, and improve bone health.
  • Better overall physical health leads to better energy levels.

How to Maintain Physical Health?

  • Commit to a healthy diet.
  • Limit snacking.
  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Move more. Take the stairs and add walking breaks to your day.
  • Get plenty of sleep.
  • Limit alcohol use. Alcohol is high in calories and may worsen health conditions common among older adults.
